# Key Findings — IBM HR Attrition Analysis

## Overall Attrition Rate
237 out of 1,470 employees left (16.1%)

## By Department
- Research & Development: 133 left (highest raw count)
- Sales: 92 left
- Human Resources: 12 left

## By Job Role
- Laboratory Technician, Sales Executive, and Research Scientist 
  together account for 70% of all attrition
- Laboratory Technician: 62 left (26.16% of total attrition)

## By OverTime (strongest finding)
- Employees who work overtime: 30.53% attrition rate
- Employees who don't work overtime: 10.44% attrition rate
- Overtime workers are roughly 3x more likely to leave
